Where's Pressflow 7?

Drupal 7 has most of the important performance and scalability features of Pressflow 6. Pressflow 7 will eventually exist, and it will generally consist of Drupal 8 backports, enhancements we'd like to roll into Drupal 8 but need sooner for big projects, and probably some simplification/removal of Drupal 7 systems that slow down large sites but help smaller ones.
